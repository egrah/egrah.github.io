Our Packages

Decor

All of our in-home or e-design interior decorating services will include a full package to transform your space.Included in this package is a breakdown of key considerations for your spaces with any necessary instructions and explanations. For example, instructions are included for e-design solutions that are being completed by the home owner or a contractor, “hang curtain rod 4-6 inches above the top of the window frame to elongate the window”.

You will also receive an itemized breakdown of each of the products sourced for your vision boards along with theirassociated costs. For our in-home or interior decorating e-design services where our clients are doing their own purchasing we also provide the links to purchase each of the products.

Floor plans are created to provide another visual that is specific to the placement of any furniture or larger items being purchased. These visuals allow our e-design clients a deeper understanding of where to place the items in theirspaces.
