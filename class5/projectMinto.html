Project First One but not the Last One Over the last few years, I have had the pleasure of working with this special client on a number of occasions, and am always honoured to keep coming back as their wish list evolves. With this project, the majority of the furniture came from a previous home but we were here to select all the hard finishes on this new build. Kitchen finishes and appliances, bathrooms, living room fireplace and all lighting and tile throughout was hand picked by our team. A special appearance of a little mermaid doll was one of the first things I ever picked up for this client a few years back and it was nice to see it reappear again in their new home.
